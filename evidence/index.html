How this library works

Checked, in the open

Accuracy

Every summary is checked against the source it cites by an independent AI. If a claim isn’t in the source, it doesn’t ship.

Usefulness

Each entry says what the evidence means for a real job — a bid, a commissioning conversation, a service design.

Values

Sources are screened for whether they belong in a library the Alliance puts its name to. People, not machines, hold this judgement.

Freshness

Links are re-checked and new sources added monthly. Entries last verified 6 July 2026.
