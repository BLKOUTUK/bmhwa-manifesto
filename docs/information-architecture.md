# Information Architecture

## Site Map

```
Home
├── The Manifesto
│   ├── Full text (anchored sections per demand area)
│   ├── Download PDF
│   └── Key statistics sidebar
├── The Case for Change
│   ├── Data & evidence (visualised)
│   ├── Personal stories
│   └── Timeline of the crisis
├── Take Action
│   ├── Sign the pledge
│   ├── Write to your MP
│   ├── Share toolkit (social media assets, email templates)
│   ├── For organisations (endorsement form)
│   └── For practitioners (resources)
├── About the Alliance
│   ├── Who we are
│   ├── Steering committee
│   ├── Partner organisations
│   └── Our journey (2022 workshops → 2024 launch → now)
├── Learning & Capacity Building
│   ├── Assess & Align tool
│   ├── Resources for the sector
│   ├── Research equity principles
│   └── Training & workshops
├── News & Updates (blog/CMS)
├── Press & Media
│   ├── Press releases
│   ├── Media kit (logos, images, key stats)
│   └── Press contact
└── Footer
    ├── Newsletter signup
    ├── Social media links
    ├── Privacy policy
    └── Contact
```

## Page Purposes

| Page | Primary Goal | Key Action |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Home | Hook → Context → Action | "Read the manifesto" |
| The Manifesto | Understand the demands | "Download PDF" / "Take action" |
| The Case for Change | Feel the urgency | "Share these facts" |
| Take Action | Convert understanding to action | Multiple paths by audience |
| About | Build trust in the coalition | "Join the Alliance" |
| Learning & Capacity Building | Show the Alliance builds, not just demands | "Use Assess & Align" |
| News | Stay informed | "Sign up for updates" |
| Press & Media | Enable media coverage | "Download media kit" |

## User Journeys

### Journey 1: Policy Audience (MP, commissioner, NHS leader)
Home → The Manifesto → The Case for Change → Take Action (for organisations)

### Journey 2: Lived Experience / Community Member
Home → The Manifesto → Take Action (sign pledge, share) → About

### Journey 3: Journalist
Home → Press & Media → The Case for Change → The Manifesto

### Journey 4: Practitioner
Home → The Manifesto → Take Action (for practitioners) → Learning & Capacity Building

### Journey 5: Researcher
Home → Learning & Capacity Building (Assess & Align) → The Case for Change

## MVP Pages (if timeline compressed)

1. **Home** — hero, manifesto summary, key stats, CTA
2. **The Manifesto** — full text, PDF download
3. **Take Action** — pledge form, share toolkit

Everything else launches post-MVP.
