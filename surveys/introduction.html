Website Redesign Proposal

A New Home for the Black Mental Health Manifesto

We've audited the current site, mapped what's possible, and designed a path to launch by April. Here's what we found — and what we need from you.

The Problem

The current site is working against the manifesto

The manifesto demands structural change to close the inequality gap for Black people within mental health services. That demands authority, reach, and clarity. The current Wix site undermines all three.

Search Engines

The manifesto is invisible to Google

Wix renders content with JavaScript. Search engines struggle to read it. Someone searching "Black mental health manifesto UK" may never find you.

Shareability

Shared links look generic

When someone shares the site on LinkedIn or WhatsApp, there's no compelling preview — no image, no description. Every share is a missed opportunity.

Design

The design doesn't match the authority of the demands

A national policy campaign needs to project weight. The current design is clean but generic — it doesn't convey the gravity of what's being demanded.

Accessibility

People using screen readers have a poor experience

Wix outputs heavily nested code with limited accessibility support. For a health equity campaign, this is a contradiction.

4x
Black people are detained under the Mental Health Act at four times the rate of white people. The manifesto demands this must end. The website should make this fact impossible to ignore.

What's Possible

A site that matches the urgency of the demands

Here's what the new site could include. Your survey responses will determine what we prioritise.

☶

The manifesto, beautifully presented

Full text with clear sections, downloadable PDF, and key statistics alongside each demand.

☰

Data that makes the case

Accessible visualisations of the evidence — detention rates, treatment orders, workforce gaps — so the numbers land.

✓

Endorsement wall

Every supporting organisation visible. Social proof that this isn't one voice — it's a movement.

✉

Write to your MP

Supporters enter their postcode, find their MP, and get a template letter. Advocacy made easy.

✎

Newsletter signup

Build a supporter base. Keep people informed. Drive action when it matters.

⚙

Press kit

Logos, key stats, press releases — everything a journalist needs to cover the campaign, in one download.

★

Learning & Capacity Building

Featuring Assess & Align and resources for the sector. The Alliance builds solutions, not just demands.

⇄

Optimised sharing

Every shared link shows a compelling preview. Key stats formatted as shareable social cards.

Plus: blog/news section for updates, event listings, pledge/endorsement forms, and proper SEO so the manifesto is findable.

What it costs

The design and build are pro bono. The only ongoing costs are hosting and domain renewal. Your survey responses will help us recommend the right option.

Option Hosting Domain Total / year
Webflow ~£20/mo ~£10/yr ~£250
Astro + Netlify £0 ~£10/yr ~£10
WordPress ~£10/mo ~£10/yr ~£130

All options include SSL, CDN, and no Wix branding. The free option (Astro + Netlify) is technically excellent — the trade-off is a slightly less intuitive content editor. Newsletter platform (Mailchimp free tier) and analytics (Plausible or Google Analytics) are free regardless.
