Black people are detained under the Mental Health Act at nearly 4 times the rate of white people.

This must end.

The Black Mental Health Manifesto demands structural change. 23 recommendations. 3 areas of action. A coalition of people and organisations saying: enough.

The Crisis in Numbers

The evidence is clear

23%

Black adults experiencing a common mental health problem weekly — compared to 17% of White British adults.

NHS Digital, 2016

6%

Black adults receiving treatment for mental health problems — compared to 13% of White British adults.

NHS Digital, 2016

3.6x

Higher detention rate under the Mental Health Act for Black people compared to white people.

NHS Digital, 2023

£118bn

Annual cost of mental ill-health to the UK economy. Addressing disparities means cost savings for everyone.

McDaid et al, 2022
