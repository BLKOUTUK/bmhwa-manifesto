Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by the Black Mental Health and Wellbeing Alliance (BMHWA). For privacy enquiries, contact us at BlackMHManifesto@gmail.com.

What data we collect

This is a static website. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that identify individual visitors. If you contact us by email or sign up for updates, we will hold the information you provide (such as your name and email address) for the purpose of communicating with you about the campaign.

How we use your data

Any personal information you provide (e.g. through a signup form or email) will only be used to:

  • Send you campaign updates and calls to action
  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Keep you informed about the manifesto and the Alliance's work

We will never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email BlackMHManifesto@gmail.com.

Third-party services

This website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify's privacy policy applies to the hosting infrastructure. We do not use Google Analytics or other tracking services.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
